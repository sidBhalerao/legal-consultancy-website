Practice Areas


We also represent both buyers and sellers in the purchase, lease, acquisition and all types of transaction concerning real estate, including commercial and industrial real estate. We have experience of representing clients in land development, and obtaining necessary permission from the government and local authorities.

We also settle disputes by Alternative Dispute Resolution Process. For Alternative Dispute Resolution we have experienced mediators in different fields.

Court Of Practice

The courts of practice include the Supreme Court of India, High Courts and District Courts.
